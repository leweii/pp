Jakob He

拍/码/踢

Hi, I'm Jakob He (雅Kob). I work at the intersection of photography and technology.

Through my camera, I explore visual narratives—freezing moments that tell stories beyond words. This portfolio is a collection of those visual experiments.

On the technical side, I build AI agents and autonomous systems. I'm particularly drawn to ReAct patterns, MCP services, and the challenge of making machines think more independently. I write about these explorations at jakobhe.com.

Both pursuits share something fundamental: finding patterns, making decisions, capturing essence. One through light and composition, the other through code and logic.
